Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

At LAWLENS Intelligence Systems ("LAWLENS", "we", "us", or "our"), security and privacy are fundamental to our architecture and operations. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, process, and protect information when you use our platform and services.

1. Enterprise Security First

We recognize that legal documents are highly sensitive. LAWLENS is built with a zero-retention foundation for unstructured legal data:

  • No AI Model Training: Your documents, agreements, and inputs are never used to train, retrain, or improve our core foundational models.
  • Ephemeral Processing: Legal documents uploaded for analysis are processed in ephemeral memory and immediately purged from compute instances following analysis generation, unless explicitly instructed by you to store them in your secure vault.
  • Data Segregation: All tenant data is strictly logically partitioned. Enterprise clients may opt for physical database separation and regional data residency.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Name, work email address, organization name, and role.
  • Authentication Data: Passwords (hashed and salted) and authentication tokens.
  • Usage Data: Analytical logs on feature utilization to improve platform reliability (stripped of any personally identifiable or client-sensitive payload context).

3. Compliance & Security Certifications

We adhere to industry-leading standards. Our infrastructure undergoes regular penetration testing and vulnerability scanning. LAWLENS holds SOC2 Type II certification and supports compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and HIPAA (upon execution of a BAA for healthcare-adjacent MSAs).

4. Data Encryption

All data exchanged with LAWLENS is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3. Any data authorized to be stored at rest is encrypted using AES-256.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our security infrastructure, or if you need to request our most recent SOC2 audit report, please contact our Information Security team at security@lawlens.app.
