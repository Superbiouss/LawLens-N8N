Got questions?

We've got answers.

Is LAWLENS suitable for sensitive client data?
Yes. Security is our foundation. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We deploy isolated, zero-retention LLM instances, meaning your documents are processed ephemerally and never used to train our AI models. We are SOC2 Type II compliant.
What types of documents can LAWLENS analyze?
While we specialize in high-stakes commercial agreements (MSAs, NDAs, DPA, SAAS agreements, Employment Contracts), LAWLENS is trained to understand the legal nuances of most corporate legal documentation, including complex nested schedules and addendums.
Can we customize the risk scoring?
Absolutely. The Enterprise tier allows your legal operations team to define custom playbooks. You can set specific limits for liability caps, govern auto-renewals, or blacklist specific governing laws, and LAWLENS will flag them according to your firm's strict guidelines.
Does it integrate with our existing CLM?
We currently integrate seamlessly via API with major CLMs like Ironclad and DocuSign CLM. We also support standard integrations with SharePoint and Google Workspace.
