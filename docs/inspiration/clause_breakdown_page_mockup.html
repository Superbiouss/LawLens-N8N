Clause 1
Confidentiality
Flagged
Original text
"The Receiving Party agrees to keep confidential all information disclosed by the Disclosing Party… for an unlimited period of time."
Plain English
You must keep everything shared by Acme Corp secret — and this obligation never ends. Even after the business relationship is long over, you're still legally bound.
No sunset clause. Industry standard is 2–5 years. An unlimited term is aggressive and difficult to negotiate out of later.
Clause 2
Obligations
Review
Original text
"The Receiving Party shall use the Confidential Information solely for evaluating a potential business partnership."
Plain English
You can only use the information for one specific purpose: deciding whether to partner with Acme. Any other use — even internally comparing them with competitors — could be a breach.
The permitted purpose is narrowly worded. Ensure your intended use falls within "evaluating a partnership" or push to broaden this definition.
Clause 3
